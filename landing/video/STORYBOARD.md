# WUDAX Launch Video Storyboard

Format: 1920×1080, 50 seconds, calm product launch film.

## Frame 1 — Mountain margin

- Duration: 0–6s
- Visual: Misty mountain texture from `design/ink_mountains.png`, slow push-in. Thin route line appears like ink on paper.
- Camera: Very slow dolly forward, no dramatic shake.
- VO: “你在山里做的每个决定，都不是一个分数能解释的。”
- Text: `真正重要的是余量`

## Frame 2 — Signals stack

- Duration: 6–13s
- Visual: Three app screenshots slide in as lightweight field notes: home, budget, check-in.
- Camera: Parallax cards, route contour background.
- VO: “路线还剩多少，水还够不够，长下坡什么时候开始，太阳多久落下，GPS 现在可信不可信。”
- Text: `路线 · 补给 · 日照 · GPS`

## Frame 3 — Local decision chain

- Duration: 13–21s
- Visual: GPX polyline, small data chips, deterministic rule engine node, endpoint “行动建议”.
- Camera: Line draw animation, then settle.
- VO: “WUDAX 把 GPX、补给、位置、身体反馈和端侧 Agent 放进同一条本地决策链。”
- Text: `离线运行 · 本地判断`

## Frame 4 — Before departure

- Duration: 21–29s
- Visual: Budget and gate screenshots. Highlight experience comparison and supply checklist.
- Camera: Soft lateral move, amber highlights.
- VO: “出发前，它告诉你这条路线和你走过最难的一次差在哪里。”
- Text: `行前认识余量`

## Frame 5 — During trip

- Duration: 29–38s
- Visual: Check-in and retreat screenshots. Risk reasons reveal one by one.
- Camera: Slight push, then hold on retreat action.
- VO: “行进中，它只在关键节点出现：长下坡前、补给下降时、时间窗口收紧时。”
- Text: `风险叠加时，行动要明确`

## Frame 6 — Hardware future

- Duration: 38–47s
- Visual: 3D knee exoskeleton render / model orbit. Material glints, no sci-fi neon.
- Camera: 120° orbit, slow and physical.
- VO: “未来，膝关节外骨骼会把真实力学状态带进这条判断链，少问你，多懂你。”
- Text: `膝关节外骨骼 · v2.0 预留`

## Frame 7 — Close

- Duration: 47–52s
- Visual: WUDAX wordmark on warm paper white. A route line resolves into the character “无”.
- Camera: Still.
- VO: “WUDAX。无待，自在前行。”
- Text: `无待 · 自在前行`
